Who am I?

Hey there, I’m Joey (or Douglas J. Honeycutt if you’re not into the whole “brevity” thing). I'm based out of Jasper, Alabama. I am a husband, a father, all-around cool guy, and making the magic box turn pretty colors makes me happy. Web development, graphic design, and programming have been my hobby and my passion for years. Now I do freelance work, but my real dream is to put my skills to work as part of a team in a professional environment.

I discovered my love of technology on a Packard Bell 486 and cut my teeth on programming with Microsoft QBasic. I’ve come a long way since then, and I’m proud to boast a knowledge and understanding of a number of tools, languages, and frameworks today, including Python, JavaScript, React, HTML5, NGINX, Blender, Photoshop, and many more.

When I'm not coding, looking after my son, or working my day job, I like to spend my time on a number of other hobbies including 3D printing, Tabletop RPGs, and Geocaching.
