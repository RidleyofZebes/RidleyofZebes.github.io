Who am I?

Hello, my name is Douglas J. Honeycutt, but everyone I know calls me Joey. I'm based out of Jasper, Alabama. I am a husband, a father, and I am also kinda good at computering. As a matter of fact, web design, development, and programming have been my hobby and my passion for years. Now I'm ready to put my skills to work in an environment where they will be rigorously tested by the standards of the professional market.

I cut my teeth on programming back in the late '90s with an old copy of Microsoft QBasic. I really didn't know what I was doing at the time, but making the magic box make pretty colors made me happy. Not long after, I discovered HTML and my parents got me a set of books on web design for Christmas that year. I read the covers off them, and by the late '00s I had a couple of web pages online I'd coded myself and paid out of pocket to host.

Since then, I've come to know and love many other languages and technologies, including Python, JavaScript, web development essentials such as PHP, SQL, and SASS, and dabbled in C++ and Node.js. Python specifically granted me an insight into programming that has allowed me to quickly acclimate to other new environments with rapid progress and absorption.

Up until now, my only real workplace experience has been an unfortunate mixture of food service and retail which, while it has paid the bills thus far, is not my preferred career path by any means. That's where you come in! If you're reading this, I'll bet you're either a recruiter or prospective client. If you like what you see here, then with your help I can escape the dreaded Retail Rut™!

When I'm not working to further develop my coding skills, I like to spend my time on a number of other hobbies including music, fantasy novels, tabletop RPGs and worldbuilding. You can catch some of my escapades around the internet at the following links:
