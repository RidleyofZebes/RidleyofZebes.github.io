Who am I?

Hello, my name is Douglas J. Honeycutt, but everyone I know calls me Joey. I'm based out of Jasper, Alabama. I am a husband, a father, all-around cool guy, and making the magic box make pretty colors makes me happy. Web development, design, and programming have been my hobby and my passion for years. Now I'm seeking to put my skills to work in an environment where they will be rigorously tested by the standards of the professional market.

I cut my teeth on programming back in the late '90s with an old copy of Microsoft QBasic. Since then, I've come to know and love many other languages and technologies. Python specifically granted me an insight into programming that has allowed me to quickly acclimate and absorb new environments with rapid progress. While my focus has since shifted toward more front-end centric languages, Python will always be my baby, my first love, and an invaluable part of my toolkit.

When I'm not coding, I like to spend my time on a number of other hobbies including music, fantasy novels, tabletop RPGs and worldbuilding. You can catch some of my escapades around the internet at the following links:
